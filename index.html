About Me

Designing with purpose

Every pixel has a reason. Every line of code serves the experience.

Hello — I'm Alex Rivera, a multidisciplinary designer and front-end developer based in San Francisco. I specialise in creating digital experiences that are not just visually refined, but deeply functional and user-centred.

With over five years in the industry, I've collaborated with startups, established brands, and creative agencies to ship products that balance aesthetic elegance with measurable business outcomes.

My approach blends systematic design thinking with a love of craft — bringing together brand strategy, interaction design, and clean front-end engineering into a single, coherent workflow.
